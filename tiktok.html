Welcome to the Tik Tok learning tool guide.

Tik Tok is a video creating platform used by over 800 million people worldwide. Tik Tok content ranges from popular Dances to video tutorials, It is used to make videos of absolutely everything! It is even used for teaching and learning!

Tik Tok is a great platform in which students can Interact with their teachers, and teachers can interact with their students. Some students maybe visual learners and may prefer different ways of learning than usual. With Tik Toks special editing features, content taught by teachers can be made more interesting for students to watch. A teachers goal is to get their student to always remember the content taught. Tik Tok is the perfect platform for such as learning could be made more fun! Tik Tok is available on the App Store and or Google Play.

Once you have installed Tik Tok, you are able to view trending Tik Toks from creators all over the world. This is available on the “For You” page. To enter the For You page you must click the “Home” option in the bottom options panel, in the “Home” page you are also able to view the Tik Toks of the creators you follow. Alongside the “Home” option there is the “Discover” option which can be used to search up any content related to the word typed into the search bar, trending hashtags and popular music is also displayed on the Discover page. Other options in the bottom panel include the “+” option which is to create a Tik Tok, the “Inbox” option which is used to message friends, and lastly the “Me” option which brings you to your own Tik Tok page. Keep in mind that only the “Home” and “Discover” pages are available for use when an account has not yet been created. It really goes to show how easily accessible the platform is for potential students!
But what if you are a teacher seeking to create content? Simple!

Sign up for Tik Tok using your Phone or Email, you also have the option to continue sign up using your Facebook, Google, Twitter or Apple account.
