Welcome to the Slack learning tool guide.

Slack is a platform available on the App Store, Google play and browser. To hold meetings in Slack a channel must be created and named, once that is done those whom you invite to your Slack channel are able to join.

Used by approx 122 thousand companies all around the world, Slack is a platform where meetings are held and where teams can work collaboratively. Slack is easy to navigate through and not as complicated as the other social media platforms considering its purpose.

A Slack channel can be either locked or open, if your Slack channel is open, any of your friends can join. You also have the option to type whilst in a Slack channel. Keep in mind, if a group wish to call on slack, a maximum of 15 members are supported per call. The video below demonstrates how a new channel is created using Slack.
