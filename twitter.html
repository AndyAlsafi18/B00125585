Welcome to the Twitter learning tool guide.

Twitter is a social media platform used by around 330 Million people worldwide! It is known for the platform where celebrities can interact with their fans. Twitter would be a good platform for teaching and learning for the reason that it includes features such as tweeting, meaning students can follow a teacher on twitter and would be able to view any tweets posted by them.

The character limit for tweeting is 140 characters. Although you cannot create private groups, direct message group chats can be created. This would be perfect for a teacher whom seeks to interact with his classes, a group can be created and any updates or general information a teacher seeks to share can be typed in a tweet for the class to see, or in some cases multiple classes to see, of course depending on who follows the teacher.
